Services

Design & Engineering Expertise

Our in-house engineering team works closely with clients to co-develop functional, durable, and performance-driven plastic parts. From concept to CAD, we ensure each component meets stringent automotive standards — both in form and function.

High-Precision Injection Molding

Leveraging advanced Japanese molding technologies, we deliver exceptional dimensional accuracy and repeatable quality at scale. Our process includes mold optimization, process tuning, and real-time quality monitoring to meet the most demanding production requirements.

OEM-Centric Customization

We specialize in producing custom plastic components for top-tier automotive OEMs including Honda, Toyota, and Suzuki. Every product is manufactured to seamlessly integrate with the client’s final assembly line, ensuring fit, finish, and function are never compromised.
